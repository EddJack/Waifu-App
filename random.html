Chitoge Kirisaki

About

Chitoge kirisaki is the main female protagonist of the Nisekoi series, written and illustrated by Naoshi Komi. She serves as the fake girlfriend of Raku Ichijo for the next three years of her school life to prevent a war from starting between their families. She is Raku's love interest.
